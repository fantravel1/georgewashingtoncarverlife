Books & Publications

Essential reading for understanding George Washington Carver's life and legacy

📚

George Washington Carver: Scientist and Symbol

by Linda O. McMurry

The definitive scholarly biography examining Carver's life and how he became a cultural icon.

Oxford University Press, 1981
📚

George Washington Carver: A Life

by Christina Vella

Comprehensive biography drawing on newly discovered letters and documents.

LSU Press, 2015
📚

George Washington Carver: In His Own Words

by Gary R. Kremer

Collection of Carver's letters, speeches, and writings that reveal his philosophy and character.

University of Missouri Press, 1987
📚

My Work Is That of Conservation

by Mark D. Hersey

Environmental biography exploring Carver's role as a pioneer of conservation and sustainability.

University of Georgia Press, 2011
📚

Unshakable Faith

by John Perry

Dual biography of Booker T. Washington and George Washington Carver at Tuskegee.

Multnomah Publishers, 1999
📚

A Weed Is a Flower

by Aliki

Classic children's biography introducing young readers to Carver's inspiring life story.

Simon & Schuster, 1965
