### **8. Myths vs Facts** â“
**File:** `learn/myths-facts.html`

**Content:**
- **MYTH:** Carver invented peanut butter
  - **FACT:** Existed before him; he popularized 300+ peanut products

- **MYTH:** Carver was born a slave and died a slave
  - **FACT:** Born into slavery, freed as infant, became renowned scientist

- **MYTH:** He held hundreds of patents
  - **FACT:** Only 3 patents; refused to patent most inventions for public benefit

- **MYTH:** He was only interested in peanuts
  - **FACT:** Also researched sweet potatoes, soybeans, cotton, and many other crops

- **MYTH:** He worked alone in isolation
  - **FACT:** Collaborated with students, farmers, and other scientists
